#slackviz

Visualize your team´s slack communication.

#slackviz by moovel

Have a deep dive intor your team`s slack communication statistics. Explore how communication works in your team and learn how communication shapes culture.

How it Works!

This tool enables you to explore your teams slack jungle by creating unique statistics. Analize your erpsonal graphs and gain insights of your very own slack team.
By integrating this tool to your slack a number of data visualizations will automatically be created for your team. See yourself!

View the live demo!

Not sure what you´re getting? We´ve created a demo #slackviu that you can explore right now!

Get started with your own team!

There are two ways to get started with your #slackviz. Either you host the tool on your own Server, or use a Heroku platform to deploy #slackviz. Don't worry, there is a readme on how this works.
If you plan to deploy the service on your own server, check out the repository and follow the steps in the readme file. Once everything is up and running, simply grant persmissions to the integration and start exploring!
