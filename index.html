#slackviz

Visualize your team´s slack communication.

#slackviz by moovel

Have a deep dive intor your team`s slack communication statistics. Explore how communication works in your team and learn how communication shapes culture.

How it Works!

This tool enables you to explore your teams slack jungle with appealing statistics. Analize unique graphs and insights of your very own slack team. By integrating this tool to your slack a number of data visualizations will automatically be created for you.

#slackviz is not only great for new team members to get an overview of the team but also for long time users to learn about whats goin on around them. See yourself!

View the live demo!

Not sure what you're getting? We've created a demo #slackviz that you can explore right now! The demodata is a completely randomized slack database. So please only use it for feature testing only. Don't waste your time trying to make sense of it.

Get started with your own team!

There are two ways to get started with your own #slackviz. Either you host #slackviz on your own Server, or use a Heroku platform to deploy #slackviz. Don't worry, there is a readme on how this works.

If you plan to deploy the service on your own server, check out the repository and follow the steps in the readme file. Once everything is up and running, simply grant persmissions to the integration and start exploring!

We´ve created six different visualizations of slack-chat data

Check out the different visualizations to explore and learn from your team´s slack jungle!

Channel Heartbeat

How much activity is there in the different channels? Find out what influential events created a buzz in different channels and receive direct links into the message archive. If you know what you're looking for you can filter by channels and look at a dedicated time frame.

OPEN IN DEMO

People Land

People Land is a cluster analysis of your team's members. Clusters are created on different parameters of similarity like joined channels. Cluster analyisis only works if you make sense to it - if done right, this will reveal a lot of lived culture thorugh communication patterns. Zoom into each cluster and try to bring them to life.

OPEN IN DEMO

Channel Land

People land also creates a cluster analysis, similar to people land. Clusters are created upon parameters channel member listening. Like above you can zoom into each cluster and bring life by making sense to the details. Similar channels will give you a great insight into how teams are aligned to eachother.

OPEN IN DEMO

Top Reactions

Ever wonder what moves your team? In Top Reactions you can find messages filtered by reactions. Maybe you missed a birthday or an important realease? You'll find them filtered by time and channel in this section.

OPEN IN DEMO

Frequent Speakers

Find out who the key contributors and communication hubs on your team are. Filtered by time and on channel basis this visualization will help you understand team and channel structures.

OPEN IN DEMO

Emoji Timeline

Feelin' good feelin' great! By collecting all emojis posted in your team the emojo timeline will help you understand the vibes of your team. Gain insights from individual channels and find out when the good times roll.

OPEN IN DEMO

A note on team stats

With #slackviz we created some fancy data visualisations about how your teams uses Slack. We tried to avoid creating graphics that imply any judgement of value or give a meaning to the numbers. We also tried to find graphics that work universially for any slack team. Some of the visualisations don't even work without a human being making sense to them. So please be careful when you do so!

Slack gives a nice examples for this: "Someone might be not using Slack for several hours during the day because they are goofing off. On the other hand, they might be away from Slack because they are concentrating very hard on the work that you want them to do. We have no way of distinguishing those cases and we advise that anyone viewing these stats be careful not to infer anything which is not in the data."
